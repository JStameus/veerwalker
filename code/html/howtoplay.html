How To Play

Currently, the game conists of two phases: text-based dialogue/storytelling where the story is told through text, and you advance the story by picking from a range of things to say or do. The other phase is turn-based combat, where you and the AI take turns rolling dice to attack one another until either side is defeated.

All game interaction can be done with the mouse. Just select the dialogue option or action you want at the bottom of the screen, in the control panel.

Encounters: To attack a target, click on it and select "Attack". If you want to skip the turn(for some reason), click "Skip Turn".
